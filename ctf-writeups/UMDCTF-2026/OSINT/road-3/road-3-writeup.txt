UMDCTF 2026 -- Road OSINT Writeup
================================================================

Flag: UMDCTF{Hanna, WY}

----------------------------------------------------------------
CHALLENGE
----------------------------------------------------------------

We were given a screenshot from a live traffic webcam and had
to identify the nearest town.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SOLUTION
----------------------------------------------------------------

Step 1: Analyze the Image
--------------------------
Extract geographic clues from the image:

  - Road type: Two-lane paved highway with a solid yellow
    centerline and white fog lines -- a rural state route,
    not an interstate

  - Terrain: Sparse sagebrush, dry grasses, tan/sandy soil
    -- semi-arid high plains or high-altitude desert

  - Topography: Rolling hills with a road cut through an
    embankment on the right side

  - Wind turbines: Visible on the ridge to the right,
    indicating a wind farm nearby

  - Water: A lake or reservoir faintly visible on the far
    left horizon

  - Camera shadow: A pole-mounted camera shadow in the
    foreground confirms this is a DOT traffic webcam feed

  - Weather: Heavy stratocumulus clouds over an open
    landscape typical of the US Mountain West

These clues point strongly to Wyoming, specifically the
corridor near wind farms and a reservoir.

Step 2: Reverse Image Search
-----------------------------
TinEye returned no results. Google Lens is worth trying
but may also not hit directly. Move on to manual OSINT.

Step 3: Identify the Road
--------------------------
The combination of:

  - Wind turbines on a ridge
  - A lake/reservoir on the left horizon
  - Two-lane rural highway cutting through rolling
    sagebrush hills

...strongly suggests WY-487, which runs through the
Medicine Bow wind farm corridor and passes near
Seminoe Reservoir.

Step 4: Wyoming DOT Camera Portal
-----------------------------------
Navigate to the WYDOT 511 camera portal:

  https://wyoroad.info/Highway/webcameras/all
    ?route=NonInterstateCameras

Or go directly to cameras by route:

  https://wyoroad.info/pls/Browse/WRR.CameraRoutesQuery

Select WY-487. There are three camera locations on
this route:

  Camera                    Milepost
  ------------------------  --------
  Medicine Bow              0.60
  Shirley Rim               37.00
  WYO 487 / WYO 77 Jct     46.03

Step 5: Match the Camera
-------------------------
Clicking through the thumbnails, the camera at
WYO 487 Shirley Rim - West matches the screenshot exactly:

  - Same hill cut on the right
  - Same open basin to the left
  - Same road geometry and signage placement
  - Wind turbines visible on the ridge

Step 6: Find the Nearest Town
------------------------------
Milepost 37 on WY-487 at Shirley Rim -- the nearest
town is Hanna, WY.

----------------------------------------------------------------
FLAG
----------------------------------------------------------------

UMDCTF{Hanna, WY}

----------------------------------------------------------------
KEY TAKEAWAYS
----------------------------------------------------------------

  - DOT camera portals are a goldmine for traffic cam OSINT
    -- most US states have public feeds

  - Wind turbines + water body were the two most distinctive
    geographic identifiers

  - When reverse image search fails, go manual: identify the
    state -> find the DOT camera portal -> filter by likely
    routes
